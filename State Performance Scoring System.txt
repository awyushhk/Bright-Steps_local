
Early Action Score
------------------
Action taken in February (Month 1): +10 points
Action delayed until March (Month 2): 0 points
Action delayed until April (Month 3): -5 points (Penalty for late intervention)
Never Actioned: -10 points (Severe penalty for neglect)

Improvement Score
-----------------
1. Base Outcome Points: 
Cured (+5), 
Under Progress (+3), 
Stagnant (0), 
Regressing (-3), 
Shifted to MEDIUM Risk (+2), 
Shifted to HIGH Risk (-5).

2. "No Action" Penalty: 
If a month passes with no action taken, that child immediately gets -2 points for the month.

3. Risk Shift Bonus/Penalty: 
If the child actually drops a risk tier (e.g., High to Medium), they get a massive +4 point bonus. If they jump from High all the way to Low, they get an +8 point bonus. Conversely, if they worsen from Low to High, they get an -8 point penalty.

Service Quality Score
---------------------
Positive Consistency: Every "Cured" or "Under Progress" log gives the state +5 points.
Negative Consistency: Every "Regressing" or "Shifted to HIGH Risk" log penalizes the state -5 points.


Total Score: The final leaderboard ranking is simply Early Action + Improvement + Service Quality.
